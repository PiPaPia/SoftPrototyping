Week 1

Inflatables

First, a sketch of any figure on paper was made. Optionally, this sketch can also be made in Illustrator, but in our project we decided to do it on paper. Thereafter, the template is transferred to baking paper. Additionally, this template will be transferred to vinyl sheets. But this time cut out twice and a little more generous. The vinyl sheets are then placed around the baking paper and placed in a hot press. Do not forget to place baking paper over and under the sheets. The vinyl sheets merge in the places where no baking paper is together. On the rest is created on an air chamber.

Material

Venyl Sheets

for the outside (2x - top+bottom)

baking paper

for the inner life (1x)

Plastic Tube

to inflate (1x)
