How To

Step 1

At first you have to work with „Fusion 360“ again. This time you will prepare your own shape for your soft robotic.

Step2

In the next step, you will take the components A and B and mix them together in a 50/50 ration. Therefor you can also use a scale the more accurate. You can also add some color, if you want let your mold look nicer.

Step 3

The next step is very important! You have to spray your shape with ??? so that your material will not stick on it and maybe get damaged.

Step 4

After that you can fill the mixed components into your shape and check if there are any air bubbles. If so, they have to be taken away with a toothpick or something similar

Step 5

Now put your shape in the oven for about 15 minutes and check if it is solid.

Step 6

In that time you can create your top part, which will be connected with the rest if your shape later in.
